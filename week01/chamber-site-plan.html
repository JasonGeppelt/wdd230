College Station Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

College Station Chamber of Commerce (cscc.org)

Site Purpose

The College Station Chamber of Commerce serves as a regional driver of economic growth, a hub for leadership, and a champion for the community. We support business growth through member services, economic and community advancement, and public policies that foster economic well-being.

Scenarios

These scenarios represent common questions our site visitors might have. They help guide the content structure of the website to ensure it meets the needs of our audience.

  • What networking events does the chamber have planned for local businesses this month? - This will require an events calendar on the site.
  • Where can I find contact details for the chamber's board of directors? - A page with board member profiles and contact options is needed.
  • What recent developments in local business regulations should I be aware of? - We will provide a news or updates section for regulatory changes.
  • How can local businesses benefit from becoming members of the chamber? - A section detailing membership benefits, resources, and support will be included.
  • What initiatives is the chamber involved in to promote local economic development? - Information about ongoing projects and partnerships will be featured prominently.

Color Schema

Using specific colors to ensure thematic consistency throughout the site:

  • Aggie Maroon (#500000): for headings and accents.
  • Cream (#FFFFCC): for text boxes and panels (as exampled in the Scenarios section).
  • Night (#0C0C0C): for primary text.
  • Onyx (#323A39): for secondary text and accents.
  • White (#FFFFFF): for a clean background.

Typography

Using 'Lato' for body text, 'Montserrat' for headings, and 'Merriweather' for special sections like quotes (as exampled below) or important callouts.
