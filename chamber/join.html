Membership Benefits

NP Membership

Our NP Membership is designed for non-profit organizations. This membership comes at no cost and includes the following benefits:

  • Access to networking events
  • Basic listing in our directory
  • Participation in community outreach programs

Bronze Membership

The Bronze Membership includes all NP benefits, plus:

  • Enhanced directory listing
  • Access to member-exclusive webinars and workshops
  • Discounts on event tickets

Silver Membership

The Silver Membership includes all Bronze benefits, plus:

  • Priority placement in our directory
  • Free admission to select events
  • Advertising opportunities in our newsletter
  • Participation in member spotlight features

Gold Membership

The Gold Membership is our premium offering, including all Silver benefits, plus:

  • Premium directory listing with logo and detailed description
  • Unlimited free event admissions
  • Top priority advertising in all chamber publications
  • Exclusive networking opportunities with top industry leaders
  • Personalized support from our membership team
